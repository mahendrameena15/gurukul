Core Subjects

Arts

A comprehensive study of the native language (e.g., Hindi, Sanskrit) with an emphasis on grammar, vocabulary, reading, and writing skills English language studies focus on literature, communication, writing, and comprehension.

Mathematics

In-depth understanding of arithmetic, algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and calculus. Problem-solving and logical reasoning techniques.

Science

Integrated study of Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with practical experiments. Environmental Science and Awareness.

Social Studies

History covering ancient civilizations, medieval times, and modern history. Geography, exploring physical, political, and economic aspects of the world. Civics, understanding the government, rights, and responsibilities of citizens.

Daily practice of yoga and meditation for physical and mental well-being.

Indian Classical Music and Dance

Training in classical music (vocal or instrumental) and traditional dance forms.

Nurture creativity through drawing, painting, and various craft projects.

Regular sports activities promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Agricultural Studies

Hands-on learning about traditional agricultural practices and sustainable farming.

Debates and Public Speaking

Computer Science and Coding
